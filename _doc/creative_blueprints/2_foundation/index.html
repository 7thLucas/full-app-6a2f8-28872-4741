Foundation Validated

Managing online inventory and customer experience efficiently

The Problem

Store owners face significant challenges in managing their online presence: scattered product information, lack of customer engagement tools, and difficulty monitoring sales performance. This results in abandoned carts, low conversion rates, and missed revenue opportunities.

The Solution

Store owners struggle with disorganized product catalogs, poor user experience, and difficulty tracking customer behavior, leading to lost sales and reduced customer retention.

Target Audience

E-commerce entrepreneurs and small-to-medium business owners in Bangladesh

Personal Motivation

Empower retailers to compete in the digital economy with professional, affordable tools
