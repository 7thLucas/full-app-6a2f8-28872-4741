# ShopCart BD — Product Overview

## Product
**Name**: ShopCart BD  
**Type**: WebView Wrapper App  
**Target URL**: https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/  

## What It Does
ShopCart BD is a dedicated webview app that launches directly into the ShopCart BD e-commerce platform at https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/. When a user opens the app, the full shopping website loads in a seamless, full-screen experience — no browser required, no URL to type.

## Core Behaviour
- On launch → immediately opens https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/ in a full-screen webview
- No additional navigation screens or splash pages beyond branded app shell
- Always loads live content from the web — any store update is instantly reflected in the app

## Users / Audience
- **Primary**: Customers of the ShopCart BD online store in Bangladesh
- Shoppers who prefer a dedicated, home-screen-bookmarked app over navigating through a mobile browser

## Positioning
A branded, app-like portal to the ShopCart BD storefront — bridging the gap between a mobile website and a native shopping app, giving customers a direct one-tap shortcut to the store from their home screen.

## Brand & Tone
- Clean, commerce-ready
- Trustworthy and direct
- Bangladeshi market focus (BDT currency, local shopper audience)

## Scope (MVP)
- Single-screen app: full-screen webview pointed at https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/
- App icon and branded splash screen
- No in-app navigation UI — the store website handles all navigation internally
