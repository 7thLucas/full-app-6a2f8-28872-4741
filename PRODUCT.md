# ShopCart BD

## Product Overview
ShopCart BD is a mobile webview wrapper app that loads the ShopCart BD e-commerce platform directly inside the app. When opened, it immediately displays https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/ in a full-screen webview — no separate browser needed. Customers use it to browse products and shop seamlessly.

## Core Feature
- Full-screen webview that opens https://shopcartbd.vercel.app/ on launch
- Clean loading state while the page loads
- Graceful back navigation handling (navigates back within webview history before exiting the app)

## Target Users
Shoppers and customers of ShopCart BD who want a native-feeling app experience for the e-commerce platform.

## Brand & Tone
- Clean, minimal, commerce-focused
- Fast and reliable feel
- No friction — gets the user to the shopping experience immediately
